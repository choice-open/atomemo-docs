## Sample Wording for Your Privacy Policy

We use **{{PRODUCT_NAME}}** by {{COMPANY_NAME}} to automate certain workflows and processes on our platform. This section explains how {{PRODUCT_NAME}} processes your information as part of our services.

### 1. What is {{PRODUCT_NAME}}?

{{PRODUCT_NAME}} is a workflow automation platform that allows us to:
- Automate data processing and business logic
- Connect various services and applications
- Execute automated workflows based on specific triggers and conditions
- Process information through custom automation rules
- Use AI agents to enhance user experiences

### 2. Information Processed by {{PRODUCT_NAME}}

When you interact with our services, {{PRODUCT_NAME}} may process:

- **User Interactions:** Actions you take on our platform that trigger automated workflows
- **Form Submissions:** Data you provide through forms that are processed by our automation workflows
- **Integration Data:** Information exchanged between our platform and third-party services through {{PRODUCT_NAME}} integrations
- **AI Processing Data:** Information processed by AI agents within workflows to provide intelligent responses and recommendations
- **Workflow Execution Logs:** Technical logs of automation activities for debugging and optimization purposes (typically stored for 14-30 days)

### 3. How We Use {{PRODUCT_NAME}}

We use {{PRODUCT_NAME}} to:

- **Automate Business Processes:** Streamline operations such as user onboarding, notifications, data processing, and report generation
- **Integrate Services:** Connect our platform with third-party services (such as email providers, CRMs, analytics tools, payment processors) to provide seamless experiences
- **Process User Requests:** Automatically handle user actions and requests through predefined workflows
- **Enhance User Experience:** Use AI agents to provide personalized recommendations, intelligent responses, and automated assistance
- **Monitor and Optimize:** Track workflow performance to improve service quality and reliability

### 4. Data Storage and Processing Location

- **Self-Hosted Deployment:** If we operate a self-hosted {{PRODUCT_NAME}} deployment, all workflow data is processed and stored on our own infrastructure under our direct control
- **{{COMPANY_NAME}}-Hosted Service:** If we use {{COMPANY_NAME}}'s hosted service, workflow execution data is processed on {{COMPANY_NAME}}'s secure infrastructure. Please refer to [{{COMPANY_NAME}}'s Privacy Policy](https://{{COMPANY_DOMAIN}}/policies/privacy-policy) for information about their data processing practices
- **Third-Party Integrations:** When workflows interact with third-party services (e.g., sending emails, storing files), data may be transmitted to those services according to the workflow configuration and the third-party service's privacy policy

### 5. Data Security

{{PRODUCT_NAME}} implements security measures including:

- **Encryption:** Data transmitted to and from {{PRODUCT_NAME}} is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (TLS/SSL)
- **Access Controls:** Strict authentication and authorization controls limit access to workflow configurations and data
- **Credential Protection:** API keys and authentication credentials used in workflows are encrypted and securely stored
- **Audit Logging:** Workflow execution activities are logged for security monitoring and compliance purposes

### 6. Data Retention

- **Workflow Configurations:** Stored as long as our account with {{PRODUCT_NAME}} remains active
- **Execution Logs:** Typically retained for 14-30 days for debugging and monitoring purposes, then automatically deleted
- **User Data in Workflows:** Retained according to our standard data retention policies and legal requirements

### 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request information about how your data is processed through our {{PRODUCT_NAME}} workflows
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data processed by our automation workflows
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations and legitimate business needs)
- **Opt-Out:** Where applicable, opt-out of certain automated processing activities
- **Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}.

### 8. Third-Party Services

Our {{PRODUCT_NAME}} workflows may connect to third-party services including but not limited to:

- Email service providers (e.g., SendGrid, Mailchimp, Amazon SES)
- Cloud storage services (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, AWS S3)
- Customer relationship management systems (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
- Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
- Communication tools (e.g., Slack, Microsoft Teams)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy and terms of service. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they handle your information.

- Explain the significance and expected consequences
- Provide information about your right to human review of the decision
