## Referral Rewards

### Rewards
- 10 credits when an invitee completes account registration (30-day validity)
- Additional 10 credits when that invitee publishes their first workflow (30-day validity)

### Referral Rules
- This program only applies to new Atomemo users who sign up through your referral link.
- Rewards are issued only after your invitee creates a new account and publishes a workflow. Incomplete or inactive referrals are not rewarded.
- We do not issue rewards for disposable or high-risk email domains. All referral emails may be reviewed to maintain high-quality participation and prevent abuse.
- Each new user can generate only one (1) referral reward event. Stacking or exploit-based reward claims are not allowed.
- Do not abuse referral links to farm credits without bringing real users. Our systems proactively monitor referral activity and trigger alerts on anomalies.
- If suspicious or policy-violating behavior is detected, we may withhold rewards or disable referral links.
- We may update, suspend, or terminate this program at any time for continuous optimization.
- For full terms, see Atomemo's [Terms of Service](/en/policies/terms-of-service).

## Credit Top-ups

### Rules
- Top-ups are available only to subscribed users
- Top-up credits are valid for 1 year
- If subscription becomes inactive during validity, top-up credits are temporarily unavailable until subscription is resumed
- Minimum purchase: 100 credits

## FAQ

### What does "Includes complimentary credits" mean?
Your subscription fee primarily pays for platform capability and service value. Credits are complimentary usage resources, not stored cash value.

### What is the difference between complimentary credits and top-up credits?
- Complimentary credits: issued through sign-up, subscription, or referral rules and expire by policy
- Top-up credits: purchased separately by subscribed users, with a standalone validity window (1 year)

### When do credits expire?
- Sign-up/subscription/referral credits: expire according to issuance rules (currently 30 days)
- Top-up credits: valid for 1 year from purchase

### Why do credits expire after cancellation?
To keep cost and incentives sustainable, credits tied to active subscription benefits (including rollovers) expire immediately when subscription is canceled or inactive. Top-up credits become usable again if subscription resumes and the credits are still valid.

### How do rollovers work after renewal?
If your subscription remains continuously active, unused complimentary credits can roll over under policy rules. If subscription is interrupted, rollover eligibility ends.

### When are referral rewards issued, and why might they not be issued?
Rewards are issued only after the invitee completes registration and publishes a workflow. No reward is issued for incomplete registration, no workflow publish, disposable email domains, or high-risk email cases.

### What behavior may trigger risk controls?
Including but not limited to: suspicious bulk referrals, non-genuine users, exploit-based reward farming, and abnormal burn patterns. We may withhold rewards or disable referral links when abuse is detected.

### Why can the same workflow consume different credits?
Credit burn depends on model pricing, input/output token volume, node invocation count, and execution rounds. More complex workloads or higher-cost models consume more credits.

### Will these rules change after open registration?
Possibly. Current policies are designed for the invite-only stage. After open registration, we may adjust plans, rewards, burn rates, and limits based on real usage, conversion, and risk-control data. Official announcements take precedence.
